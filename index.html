The History of Chaaye Station

Chaaye Station was conceived in our minds long before it was established five years ago in F-6 Islamabad. The idea to have a tea-centric cafe in a nation, that consumes more tea per capita than any other country, grew stronger with time and in the absence of any viable hang-out options for the professionals, the housewives, the family or simply someone who loves a good book with their tea.

The success of chaaye station is as much a result of the gap that existed in the marketplace as it is of the careful planning that went into designing the right menu, the right space for our customers and the right training of our staff. From the seventy different blends of tea to the ever evolving food menu to the music that plays all day long; everything is personally selected. This personal involvement in all things chaaye station is the bedrock on which it was built and continues to operate to this day.

A brand that was established for the love of tea has come a long way since it’s inception. Chaaye station now serves customers in several outlets in Pakistan and continues to grow. As the chaaye station brand grows, our franchise team is inundated with queries for more franchises around the country and more recently, from other parts of the world.
